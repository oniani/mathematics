Use UNITS with residual standard error.

Our goal is not to have any correlation among predictor variables.
Centering around the mean trick is aimed at fixing this issue.

R^2 always goes up when adding more predictors.
Adjusted R^2 "adjusts" for the number of predictors - adds a penalty.

Generally speaking, high p-values mean correlation between predictor variables.

Multicoliniarity is a fancy word for correlation among predictor variables.
